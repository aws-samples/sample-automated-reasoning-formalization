# Company Expense Approval Policy

This document describes the rules governing expense report submission and approval at Acme Corp.

## Submission Eligibility

All full-time employees may submit expense reports. Contractors may not submit expense reports. Interns may submit expense reports only if their manager co-signs the submission.

## Approval Thresholds

- Expenses of $100 or less do not require approval and are automatically reimbursed.
- Expenses over $100 and up to $5,000 require manager approval.
- Expenses over $5,000 require both manager approval and finance department approval.

## Travel Expenses

Travel expenses follow the same approval thresholds as other expenses. Additionally:

- Domestic travel expenses are reimbursed at 100% of the approved amount.
- International travel expenses are reimbursed at 100% of the approved amount, but require finance department approval regardless of the amount.

## Meal Allowances

- The daily meal allowance for domestic travel is $75.
- The daily meal allowance for international travel is $100.
- Meal expenses exceeding the daily allowance require a written justification.

## Reimbursement Timing

- Approved expenses under $1,000 are reimbursed within 5 business days.
- Approved expenses of $1,000 or more are reimbursed within 15 business days.

## Receipt Requirements

- All expenses over $25 require an itemized receipt.
- Expenses of $25 or less may be submitted without a receipt.
